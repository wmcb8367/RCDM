## Evolution

- **v1 → v1.1:** tiny cleanup pass, essentially the same manuscript.
- **v1.1 → v1.2:** major trimming and tightening.
- **v1.2 → v1.3:** another substantive edit pass.
- **v1.3 → v1.4:** no word-count change, so likely formatting or sentence-level refinement.
- **v1.4 → v1.6:** most substantial restructuring within the v1 branch. More headings and finer chapter breakdown.
- **v1.6 → v2:** clear edition jump, not just another cleanup. New material added back in, structure modernized.
- **v2 → v2.1:** light polish and incremental additions.

## Structural Snapshot

### v1 branch
- Preface
- Introduction

- Fewer top-level sections
- Heavier, longer early drafts that are progressively reduced

### v1.6

### v2 / v2.1
- Adds **Prologue: The Education of a Coach**

  - Strategy

- Presents the most mature structure of the book
